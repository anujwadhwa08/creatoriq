Cadence Decision Logic
⚡ velocity_check

Trigger: New video uploaded AND within 48 hours

Frequency: Every 4 hours during the window

Agents: Performance · Audience · Prediction · Synthesis

📋 daily

Trigger: Last daily report > 20 hours ago

Frequency: Once per day

Agents: Performance · Audience · Prediction · Synthesis

📅 weekly

Trigger: Monday + last weekly > 6 days ago

Frequency: Once per week

Agents: All 5 — including SEO & Originality
